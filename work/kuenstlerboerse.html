Künstlerbörse

Art & music, raw and real.

RoleFounder & Curator
Year2024 — 2026
DisciplineCuration · Design · Communication
Linkkuenstlerboerse.net ↗

A platform for emerging artists — no gallery walls, no gatekeeping.

Künstlerbörse began as the revival of a 90s Hamburg format and grew into a movement: vernissages with house beats, creatives meeting collectors, art treated as something living rather than precious.

I founded it and run it end to end — curation, spatial concept, visual identity and communication, all from one hand. Each edition is built around a location, from a private villa to the raw halls of the Schanzenhöfe.
